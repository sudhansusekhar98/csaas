# Quickstart: Personnel Sample Activity Tracking

## Scenario 1 — View an active operator's full sample history

1. Navigate to **Personnel Management** (Authority & Logistics)
2. In the Personnel Registry table, observe each row now has four small count chips (Parent · Child · Lab · NCR) showing how many samples that operator has touched
3. Click on **Marcus Thorne (USR-8821)** — his row highlights in indigo
4. A "Sample Activity" panel expands at the bottom of the page
5. The **Parent Samples** tab is active by default — shows the parent samples where Marcus generated the QR code at the Wagon/Tripper (Sample ID, Seal ID, Colliery, Weight, Time)
6. Click the **Child Samples** tab — shows the child bag IDs Marcus handled in the Prep Room
7. Click the **Lab Receipts** tab — shows samples Marcus confirmed receipt for as Lab Technician, with acceptance status
8. Click the **NCR Filings** tab — shows any NCRs Marcus submitted, with stage, sample ID, and reason summary

## Scenario 2 — Operator with no activity in a category

1. Click on **Elena Rodriguez (USR-9044)** in the registry
2. Activity panel opens; NCR Filings tab shows "No NCR filings on record" empty state
3. Parent Samples chip in the registry row shows `—` (em-dash) for that category

## Scenario 3 — Suspended operator activity audit

1. Click on **David Kim (USR-7732)** (status: Suspended)
2. Activity panel shows all historical activity even for suspended operators
3. Lab Receipts tab shows samples received before suspension, providing an audit trail
